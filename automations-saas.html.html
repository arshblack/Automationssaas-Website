Our Story

Two Engineers.
One Brotherhood.
One Mission.

In 2020, Arshly Phiri and Alison Phiri arrived in Poland as Computer Engineering students — two Africans thousands of kilometres from home, who found in each other a reason to push further than they thought possible.

Years of late nights building real things forged engineers who don't just understand technology — they understand what it means to build for Africa.

"We came to Poland to get the skills to build something real — and bring it home."

— Arshly & Alison Phiri, Co-Founders
